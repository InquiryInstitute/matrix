Matrix rooms for how Castalia actually works

Element connects you to our homeserver for real-time chat, voice, and structured collaboration—whether you’re in office hours, a board meeting, a lecture, or a salon debate.

Ways we use Element & Matrix

Each pattern uses rooms, threads, and spaces differently—same underlying tool, tuned to the kind of inquiry you’re doing.

Live

Office hours

Drop-in rooms for faculty and members: quick questions, follow-ups, and links to readings—without losing the thread.

Governance

Board meetings

Agendas, motions, and side-channels stay in Matrix—searchable history and clear participation across time zones.

Teaching

Lectures & seminars

Course spaces for announcements, Q&A, and breakout topics alongside your LMS and materials elsewhere on Castalia.

Public program

Symposia

Multi-session programs with dedicated rooms, guests, and archives so conversations outlive a single event.

Culture

Salons

Long-form discussion and close reading—rooms that reward slower, higher-signal exchange than open social feeds.

Contention

Debates & structured dialogue

Moderated formats with clear roles: propositions, rebuttals, and a record participants can revisit.
